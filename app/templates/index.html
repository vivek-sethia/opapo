About OPAPO

OPAPO is an on-page tool to help e-commerce providers to optimize their product advertising. It analyzes e-commerce websites based on objective determinants to subsequently suggest recommendations based on features such as reviews and descriptions.

The application is based on a web-crawler that extracts useful features, followed by analysis that includes techniques such as Opinion Mining. The tool works for e-commerce sites Amazon , Ebay and Rakuten.
